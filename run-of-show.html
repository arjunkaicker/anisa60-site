The Evening Unfolds

The evening begins slowly.

Cocktails. Conversation. Music drifting through the room. Familiar faces and new connections.

As the night unfolds, unexpected moments — of art, dining, music, and celebration — surrounded by a community that has shaped this journey.

Studio 60 is designed not simply as a party —

but as a shared experience rooted in creativity, connection, generosity, and joy.

Come ready to celebrate, reflect, laugh, dance, and create unforgettable memories together.

The Lineup

Violinist

Alex Ahn

Classically trained in Atlanta, Alex Ahn has played violin for over three decades. Over time he’s evolved beyond the rigid confines of classical music into a free-flowing style inspired by Atlanta’s vibrant culture — continually redefining what the instrument can be.

Greeter & DJ

TAYLOR ALXNDR

TAYLOR ALXNDR (they/she) is an underground drag icon, musician and community organizer. Co-founder of Southern Fried Queer Pride — empowering Black and QTPOC communities across the South — and Mother of the House of ALXNDR, their art and performances are extensions of their organizing, building inclusive spaces for marginalized folks to gather and thrive.

Artist in Residence

Lisette Correa

Atlanta muralist Lisette Correa, aka Arrrt Addict, weaves her Taino and Puerto Rican heritage into vibrant, community-focused work seen across the Beltline, Krog Street Tunnel and Little Five Points. She’ll be onsite all evening, painting a living tribute that grows with every guest’s contribution.

A Living Tribute

As part of Anisa’s celebration, we are excited to share a unique and meaningful experience that will take place during the event.

Lisette Correa aka Arrrt Addict will be onsite creating a large-scale interactive artwork that will serve as a lasting tribute to Anisa and the many lives she has touched. Throughout the evening, each guest will have the opportunity to contribute to this one-of-a-kind piece. Whether through a brushstroke of paint, a written message, a meaningful symbol, or something that reminds you of Anisa, your contribution will become part of the final masterpiece.

This collaborative artwork will reflect the creativity, memories, relationships, and impact that have shaped Anisa’s journey over the years. We encourage everyone to participate and leave their personal mark on this special keepsake.

We look forward to seeing the beautiful story that unfolds through your contributions.
