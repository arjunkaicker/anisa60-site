The Evening Unfolds

The evening begins slowly.

Cocktails. Conversation. Music drifting through the room. Familiar faces and new connections.

As the night unfolds, unexpected moments — of art, dining, music, and celebration — surrounded by a community that has shaped this journey.

Studio 60 is designed not simply as a party —

but as a shared experience rooted in creativity, connection, generosity, and joy.

Come ready to celebrate, reflect, laugh, dance, and create unforgettable memories together.
