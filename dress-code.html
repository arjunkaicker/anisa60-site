For her

Cocktail · Reimagined

A black cocktail or midi dress with metallic accents — sequined, beaded, or shimmering jacquard. Statement earrings, a sparkling clutch, and heels you can dance in.

For him

Sharp · Polished

A black suit or tuxedo with subtle metallic details — gold cufflinks, a satin lapel, a silk pocket square in champagne or platinum tones.

  • Black suit or tuxedo
  • Crisp black or charcoal shirt
  • Gold or silver tie / pocket square
  • Polished black dress shoes

A friendly note — please avoid jeans, sneakers, and casual t-shirts. When in doubt, lean dressier.
