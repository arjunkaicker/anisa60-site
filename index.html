A note from Anisa

Sixty feels less like a milestone and more like an opening.

A new canvas. A new chapter. A deeper appreciation for community, creativity, resilience, and joy.

This evening is about the people who have inspired me, challenged me, supported me, collaborated with me, and walked beside me through every season.

I wanted to create something intentional — a space filled with music, conversation, artistry, laughter, and connection.

Not simply to celebrate the past. But to celebrate what's still possible.

Thank you for being part of my story. I cannot wait to celebrate together.
